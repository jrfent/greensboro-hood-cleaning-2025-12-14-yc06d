Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our hood cleaning services and process.

How often should my kitchen hood be professionally cleaned?

The frequency of professional hood cleaning depends on your cooking volume and type of food preparation. High-volume restaurants with heavy grease production should have their hoods cleaned monthly, while moderate-use kitchens may only need quarterly cleaning. Low-volume operations might require semi-annual cleaning. We recommend consulting with your insurance provider and local health department, as they may have specific requirements. Our team can assess your kitchen and recommend an optimal cleaning schedule tailored to your specific needs.

What is included in your hood cleaning service?

Our comprehensive hood cleaning service includes thorough cleaning of the hood canopy, filters, ductwork, and exhaust system. We remove all grease buildup, degreasing agents, and contaminants using our eco-friendly cleaning solutions. The service includes inspection of all components for damage or wear, replacement of disposable filters if needed, and detailed documentation of the work completed. We also provide a certification of completion for compliance and insurance purposes. Our team ensures everything is properly reassembled and functioning optimally before we leave your kitchen.

Can you work around my restaurant's operating hours?

Absolutely! We understand that your restaurant's operations are critical to your business. We offer flexible scheduling including early morning, late evening, and overnight cleaning services to minimize disruption to your kitchen operations. We can work before you open, after you close, or even during slow periods if that works best for your schedule. We also offer emergency 24-hour service for urgent situations. Simply contact us to discuss your preferred timing, and we'll do our best to accommodate your needs while ensuring thorough, professional service.

Are your cleaning products safe for food preparation areas?

Yes, safety is our top priority. We exclusively use eco-friendly, food-safe cleaning products that are approved for use in commercial kitchens. Our cleaning solutions effectively remove grease and buildup without harsh chemicals that could contaminate food preparation surfaces. All products are non-toxic and environmentally responsible, protecting your staff, customers, and the environment. We follow all health and safety regulations and best practices for food service establishments. After cleaning, we thoroughly rinse and sanitize all surfaces to ensure they're ready for food preparation.

What documentation do you provide for insurance and compliance purposes?

We provide comprehensive documentation for every service including a detailed cleaning report, certification of completion, and photographic evidence of work completed. This documentation is essential for insurance claims, health inspections, and fire code compliance verification. We maintain records of all services performed on your system, creating a complete maintenance history for your kitchen. Our certifications are recognized by insurance companies and health departments, helping you meet all regulatory requirements. We can also provide recommendations for future maintenance based on the condition of your hood system.
